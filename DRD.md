# 🎨 DRD — Design Requirements Document

If design feels cheap, trust collapses instantly.

---

## 1️⃣ Visual Style

Minimal  
Modern  
Emotionally powerful  
Professional  
Clean whitespace

No template look.  
No stock-photo overload.

---

## 2️⃣ Color Palette

Deep Blue: `#0B1F3A`  
Soft Beige: `#F6F1E9`  
White: `#FFFFFF`  
Subtle Gold Accent: `#C6A75E`  
Muted Text Gray: `#6B7280`

CTA:  
Gold or deep blue only.  
No rainbow buttons.

---

## 3️⃣ Typography

Headings:

* Poppins / Inter
* Bold, confident

Body:

* Inter / Open Sans
* 16–18px

Hero Heading:  
48–64px desktop

---

## 4️⃣ Spacing System

8px grid  
Section padding: 80–120px  
Card radius: 16px  
Buttons radius: 12px

Consistent spacing = premium feel.

---

## 5️⃣ Motion & Interaction

* Impact counter animation
* Fade-in scroll sections
* Smooth hover on cards
* 200–300ms transitions
* Subtle CTA hover lift

No flashy animation. This is NGO, not crypto.

---

## 6️⃣ Image Guidelines

Use:

* Real beneficiary photos
* Real events
* Real volunteers

Every image:

* Optimized
* Alt text added
* Emotionally authentic

---

## 7️⃣ Trust Elements Placement

Above the fold:

* Tagline
* Clear CTA

First scroll:

* Impact stats

Second scroll:

* Transparency + reports

Trust must appear early.
